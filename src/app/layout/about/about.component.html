About Us

My name is Tymia Rutherford and I'm the founder of Luscious Lips 901 by T&T. My brand was created to promote healthy skin year round. I wanted to build a brand based around all natural and organic products because I struggled with eczema mostly all of my childhood, and I know so many others in the world struggle with dry skin or other severe skin allergies. My mission is to provide exceptional skin care results to anyone who supports the Luscious Brand. Each product is made to soften, nourish, moisturize, and replenish the skin. We are always adding new items to our brand to give you the best shopping experience possible. I always want to do my best to ensure that I satisfy each and every one of my customers!
